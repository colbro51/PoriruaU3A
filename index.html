Welcome To Mana U3A (Inc)

Our group is part of an international organisation founded in France in 1973 and started in New Zealand in 1985 with the aim of providing a learning for life structure specifically for those in the older age group who wish to continue to learn and have fun socially. Mana U3A was established in 1991. We confer no degrees but learn and join interest groups for the fun of it. Our current membership of some 140 comes from Tawa to Kapiti.

Our annual programme includes four quarterly meetings which normally feature a guest speaker. In addition, members variously participate in interest groups. These groups generally meet in the friendly atmosphere of private homes with individual members actively involved in organising or leading groups. We also have occasional day trips or visits which are of interest to members.

A current annual membership costs $10 pa. There are no course fees as such but the agreed costs of any groups are shared by the course participants.
